ADMISSIONS

The entry level admission into the School is Class I. The Class I admission notification will be published in widely read English & Vernacular newspapers during October /November months of every year. The Admission process starts with issuing Application Forms on payment of Rs. 500/- on producing the original Date of Birth certificate along with a True Copy after the admission notification. The Academic year begins in April. Admission to Class IX & X is possible only if the previous school of study is CBSE affiliated. For any other class other than those mentioned above the admission is against a vacancy and a draw of lots.

Age Criteria for Admission:

A candidate seeking admission into Class I should be between 5 and 6 years of age and cut off date for calculation of age is 31st May of the year of seeking admission. Age for the other classes should be calculated accordingly.

Admission Procedure for Boarding:

Admissions are carried out on merit as well as availability of room in the hostel on payment of a separate boarding fee.
